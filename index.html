Why Patna ?

History

Patna is one of the most oldest living city of India . Earlier Patna is known as Patliputra .Patliputra was capital of Ashoka the great . He was one of the greatest king of all time. Patna holds a unique place in history. Many great leaders, scholars belonged to Patna. During the freedom struggle, Bihar has some greatest revolutionizers like Khudiram Bose, Prafulla Chaki, and Chandrasekhar Azad and it was always the honour of this place.

Culture

Bihar is a state with the culturally most mixed population. We have Hindus, Muslims, Jain, Buddhists, Sikhs, Christians living here. Bihar has a rich cultural background to be proud of it is associated with ‘Samudra Manthan’ of the Vedic era, Sita of Rama, and Karna of Mahabharata. People are shy, honest, helping, and confident of themselves.People of Bihar celebrate festivals and religious events with great happiness and love.

Cuisine

The cuisines for people of Bihar are both vegetarian and non-vegetarian. Non- vegetarianism is still less due to Buddhism and Jainism beliefs, as Buddhism and Jainism promote vegetarianism. Roti, Daal, Sabji are the basic food of Biharis. People of Bihar are fond of eating Litti Choka, some of the popular Bihari dishes are Bihari Kebab, Litti-Chokha, Bihari Boti, Bihari Chicken Masala, Sattu Paratha, Chokha, and fish curry.
